## [3.0.0] - 2024-01-09

First release as Gruvbox theme, forked from catppuccin/tmux v2.1.3.

### Changed

- Complete rebrand from Catppuccin to Gruvbox color palette
- Renamed all `@catppuccin_*` options to `@gruvbox_*`
- Renamed all `@ctp_*` internal variables to `@grv_*`
- Updated installation paths from `gruvbox/tmux` to `tmux-gruvbox`

### Removed

- Latte, Frappe, Macchiato flavors (consolidated to single dark theme)
